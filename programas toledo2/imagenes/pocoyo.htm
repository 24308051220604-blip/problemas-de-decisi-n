About pocoyo

About Pocoyo

Meet Pocoyo - the playful, inquisitive, adventurous boy in blue. Just like you, Pocoyo faces all kinds of feelings every day. Sometimes he's excited, sometimes he's curious, and sometimes he's a little bit nervous. But no matter what, Pocoyo knows that with the support of his friends, he can overcome any challenge and enjoy every adventure. Fun fact: Pocoyo means "little me" in Spanish, a perfect name for our tiny hero who's always ready for big fun, just like you!

#ELLY

About ELLY

About Elly

Elly embodies everything she aspires to be - a graceful ballerina, an impressive weightlifter, and an up-and-coming filmmaker. She can be as elegant as a princess and as tough as a warrior. Elly is also the inventor of a new Olympic discipline she calls Ballet-Karate.

#PATO

About Pato

About Pato

Pato is an intelligent duck who likes the simple things in life. While Pocoyo is big on adventure, Pato prefers to read a book or water his plants. Still, Pato and Pocoyo are best friends, proving that opposites attract. Pato is cautious and easily scared, but thanks to (or because of) Pocoyo, he finds himself stepping out of his comfort zone and into thrilling adventures.

About Loula

About LOULA

Loula is Pocoyo’s pet dog who, unlike the other animal characters, acts like an actual animal. She walks on all fours, is sweet and playful, and is always up for adventure.

About pulpo

Pulpo is a wild, opera-singing octopus who nobody understands but everybody loves. Pulpo is really bananas but always knows what he is doing, especially when it comes to cheering up Pocoyo and the rest.

About Babybird & sleepybird

Sleepy Bird is constantly found sleeping up in a tree. Now and again, he opens his eyes to see what everyone is up to, but then he drifts back to sleep, often embarking on magnificent adventures in his dreams.

About Valentina

About Valentina

Meet Valentina, the charming caterpillar who brightens everyone's day with her kindness and cuddles. Although she might not always understand every situation, her big heart and warm nature make her a beloved friend. Valentina has a huge appetite, and it's a wonder how she can fit so much food into her tiny body.
